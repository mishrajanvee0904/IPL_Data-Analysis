## 📊 Key Analytical Insights & Takeaways

1. TOSS IS OVERRATED
   Toss winners win only 51% of matches.
   → Teams should stop obsessing over toss 
     and focus on execution instead.

2. 175 IS THE MAGIC NUMBER  
   Teams scoring 175+ win 70%+ of matches 
   when defending.
   → Target 175 as minimum par score 
     in every match.

3. POWERPLAY DECIDES THE GAME
   Correlation between powerplay score and 
   final total = 0.73 (strong)
   → Invest in explosive openers — first 6 
     overs predict the entire innings.

4. DEATH BOWLING IS THE REAL DIFFERENTIATOR
   Top 3 death over bowlers have economy 
   under 8 vs bottom 3 at 12+
   → A specialist death bowler is worth 
     more than a fifth batting option.

5. EXTRAS DON'T COST MATCHES
   Winners and losers concede almost equal 
   extras (8.0 vs 8.1)
   → Discipline in extras is NOT a 
     differentiating factor in IPL.
